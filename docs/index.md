## What CreatureCapture Does

- Adds a special Capture Bow that can appear in the enchantment table.
- Lets players capture supported creatures and receive their spawn eggs.
- Tracks each player's unique captures as a persistent collection.
- Provides collection, progress, remaining-creatures, and leaderboard commands.
